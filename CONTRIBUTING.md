# CONTRIBUTING

# Contributing to the Living Civilization Framework (LCF)

Thank you for your interest in contributing.

LCF is an open framework focused on exploring ideas related to:

- Human development
- Civilization systems
- Education
- Sustainability
- Technology
- Long-term thinking

Contributions are welcome from different perspectives.

The goal is not to defend a fixed answer.

The goal is to improve understanding through discussion, evidence, experimentation, and refinement.

---

# Ways to Contribute

You can contribute through:

## Ideas and Discussion

Examples:

- Suggesting improvements
- Identifying weaknesses
- Proposing alternative viewpoints
- Asking questions

Constructive criticism is valuable.

---

## Documentation

Help improve:

- Clarity
- Structure
- Explanations
- Examples
- References

Good documentation helps others understand the framework.

---

## Research and Analysis

Contributions may include:

- Related research
- Historical examples
- Case studies
- Comparative analysis
- Alternative perspectives

---

## Technical Contributions

If the repository includes technical components, contributions may include:

- Code improvements
- Tools
- Data organization
- Automation

---

# Contribution Principles

## Respectful Collaboration

Contributors should:

- Discuss ideas respectfully
- Separate criticism of ideas from criticism of people
- Welcome different viewpoints

---

## Evidence and Reasoning

Strong contributions should include:

- Clear reasoning
- Supporting information where possible
- Practical examples

---

## Continuous Improvement

LCF is intended to evolve.

Contributions may:

- Improve existing concepts
- Challenge assumptions
- Add new perspectives
- Identify limitations

---

# Pull Requests

When submitting changes:

Please consider:

- Is the change clear?
- Does it improve the framework?
- Does it maintain consistency with existing documents?
- Does it avoid unsupported claims?

Maintainers may request changes or discussion before accepting contributions.

---

# Content Guidelines

Please avoid:

- Personal attacks
- Harassment
- Unsupported claims presented as facts
- Attempts to force a single viewpoint

LCF welcomes exploration and discussion.

---

# Attribution and Licensing

By contributing to this repository, you agree that your contributions may be distributed under the project's license.

Current project license:

[LICENSE](./LICENSE)

Contributions should respect:

- Existing copyrights
- Proper attribution
- Open-source principles

---

# AI-Assisted Contributions

AI tools may be used for:

- Brainstorming
- Drafting
- Editing
- Research assistance

However, contributors remain responsible for:

- Reviewing generated content
- Checking accuracy
- Ensuring appropriate attribution

AI-generated material should not be submitted without human review.

---

# Contributor Responsibility

Contributors are responsible for ensuring their submissions:

- Are original or properly attributed
- Do not violate third-party rights
- Follow applicable laws and licenses

---

# Final Note

LCF is a living framework.

The strength of the project comes from continuous learning, careful discussion, and contributions from people with different experiences and perspectives.

Thank you for helping improve it.
