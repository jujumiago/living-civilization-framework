# Contributing

Thank you for your interest in the Living Civilization Framework (LCF).

This project is intended to be a living framework that evolves through discussion, evidence, practical experience, and continuous improvement.

---

## Guiding Philosophy

The goal is not to defend a fixed ideology.

The goal is to improve the framework through constructive collaboration.

Contributors are encouraged to:

* Question assumptions
* Identify weaknesses
* Propose improvements
* Provide evidence
* Share historical examples
* Test ideas in practice

---

## Types of Contributions

### Research

* Historical studies
* Social development models
* Educational systems
* Governance structures
* Economic frameworks

### Framework Improvements

* New principles
* Better definitions
* Roadmap refinements
* Governance improvements

### Case Studies

* Community projects
* Organizational practices
* National development examples

### Documentation

* Writing improvements
* Clarifications
* Translations
* Visualizations

---

## Contribution Standards

Contributions should aim to be:

* Respectful
* Evidence-based
* Constructive
* Transparent
* Reproducible where possible

---

## Discussion Process

When proposing changes:

1. Describe the problem.
2. Explain the proposed solution.
3. Provide supporting evidence or reasoning.
4. Discuss potential risks and trade-offs.

---

## Continuous Improvement

No part of the framework is considered final.

Every principle, roadmap stage, and governance mechanism may be refined as new understanding emerges.

The framework grows through learning.

---

## Mission

Help humanity build stronger pathways from:

Survival → Capability → Contribution → Growth → Exploration → Legacy

for current and future generations.
