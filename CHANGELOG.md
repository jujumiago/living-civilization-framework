This document summarizes the public development history of the Living Civilization Framework (LCF).

It records significant public releases and major framework changes rather than every individual edit or internal revision.

The framework is intended to evolve over time through research, discussion, practical experience, and community contributions.

# v0.1

### Initial Release

Established the initial public release of the Living Civilization Framework, including:

- Vision
- Principles
- Roadmap
- Governance concepts

This version introduced the core development pathway:

```text
Survival
    ↓
Capability
    ↓
Contribution
    ↓
Growth
    ↓
Exploration
    ↓
Legacy
```

and established LCF as an open conceptual framework intended for discussion, research, and continuous refinement.

## Versioning Philosophy

LCF follows an incremental versioning approach.

New versions may:

- Clarify concepts
- Improve documentation
- Incorporate research
- Respond to constructive feedback
- Expand the framework while maintaining conceptual consistency

Version numbers indicate the evolution of the framework rather than completion.

Earlier versions remain part of the documented development history and may differ from later versions as concepts are refined, expanded, or revised.

The framework is expected to continue evolving as knowledge, experience, and collaboration grow.
