# Living Civilization Framework Systems Map

## Relationship Between LCF Documents and Existing Knowledge

---

## 1. Purpose

The Living Civilization Framework (LCF) connects ideas from multiple areas of human knowledge.

This document explains conceptual alignment between LCF systems and existing fields of study.

LCF does not claim to replace, copy, or represent the complete work of any individual thinker.

These connections represent areas where ideas share similar principles, questions, or directions.

---

# 2. Human Development System

## Related Field

Human psychology and development

## Alignment

LCF:

```text
Survival
    ↓
Capability
    ↓
Contribution
    ↓
Growth
    ↓
Legacy
````

explores how humans and civilization develop over time.

This aligns with human development theories that study progression from basic needs toward higher capability.

## Related Thinker

### Abraham Maslow

Alignment:

* Human needs as a foundation
* Development beyond survival
* Higher human potential

Difference:

LCF expands the idea from individual development into civilization-scale development.

# 3. Education System

## Related Field

Educational philosophy

## Alignment

LCF views education as:

* Capability development
* Lifelong learning
* Preparation for meaningful contribution

Rather than only:

* Information transfer
* Memorization
* Credential collection

## Related Thinker

### John Dewey

Alignment:

* Learning through experience
* Education connected to real life
* Development through action

Difference:

LCF connects education directly to civilization evolution.
